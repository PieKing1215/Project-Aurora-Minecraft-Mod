Aurora GSI for Minecraft

Aurora GSI (Game State Integration) for Minecraft is a Forge mod that allows Minecraft to communicate with the Aurora lighting software and send it variables to change lighting effects.

Want to have your number keys as your health bar? Want the background of your keyboard to change based on the time of day in-game? Want the keyboard to light orange when you're on fire? You can do all this and more with Aurora.
